Is it time?

03-10-2012
By Karan Luthra

In Misc.

tags: inspirational

Is it time, for me to close my eyes? To use the switch, to turn it off? Seems like the closing of the eyes, would only trigger a tear to fall Seems like, taking a breather, would only make me regret the call

Is it time, to step aside? To take the seat, and watch the game? Seems like its a runway on tight schedule, Take off, right now! Or else, dont blame. Remind me of the player I wanted to be, then anyone less, I wouldnt want to see!
